A platform for better science

The EMBL-EBI Cloud Portal is platform for cloud-based data analysis that simplifies the experience for researchers when running their workloads across different cloud providers.

The portal enables cloud providers and research teams to package and share their key applications and data environments with their respective users. That way, these key applications can be deployed on demand onto the cloud providers the user is able to access.

At a glance:

  • Deploy domain-specific applications and infrastructure, from your own repos or from the EBI Cloud Portal community.

  • Use your own or shared cloud resources.

  • Scale when you need to, securely.

The EBI Cloud Portal has been developed by the Technology and Science Integration team at EMBL-EBI.

How to access to the EMBL-EBI Cloud Portal?

The EMBL-EBI Cloud Portal is offered as a service to ELIXIR and other communities. Users will need a freely available ELIXIR identity to access the portal.

Obtain your ELIXIR identity here. Then you can login.

How to use the EMBL-EBI Cloud Portal?

Access to the portal does not provide access to any cloud providers but is compatible with the work within the ELIXIR Compute Platform. Users will need to provide their own credentials to access cloud providers such as OpenStack, Google Compute Engine or Amazon Web Services.

Research groups can use the portal to share appliances and cloud credentials within their teams through the portal if they desire.

Further questions?

Further information on the design and documentation on how to use and contribute appliances to the portal is available. Any questions around the EMBL-EBI Cloud Portal and its use can be sent to tsi[at]ebi.ac.uk.
