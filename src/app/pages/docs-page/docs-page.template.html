Introduction

This section is to help you to use the portal demonstrated with help of screenshots.

Welcome Page

This is the usual welcome or start up page. To the top right corner you will find 'Login' link.

Elixir Registration

Register with ELIXIR: https://www.elixir-europe.org/register, using your home institution or Google, etc.
This involves email verification, and finishes with a "We've got your registration" message from ELIXIR.
When you first log in to the EBI Cloud Portal, you will first be asked if you allow your home institution (or Google, etc)
to release your data to ELIXIR, where you can choose to allow/disallow the release of your data to the EBI Authentication Gateway.

I. Login using username/password

If you have an account, use the username/password combination to login, else make use of the SSO (single sign on) button.

II. Login using SSO

Click on 'SSO', you can sign in using Google, Linkedin, ORCID accounts or your institution login.

Say for example, you have entered your institution name, you will be taken to another page,
where you enter your login details you use in you institution to as follows



Enter the login details and click on 'Login'.

Profile Page

Click on ' ', a side navigation bar appears as follows

Click on 'Profile' which will then take you to profile page where you can add, share and remove Cloud Credentials and Teams.

I. Cloud providers

To add Cloud Providers, click on the 'Cloud Providers' tab in you Profile Page.
To the right is a '+' symbol which will allow you to add a Cloud Provider.


You can add Cloud Provider Parameters , by clicking on the '+' next to the Parameters. To add
more parameters just click on '+'. Then hit on 'Save' button to save the information.
You will be able to see the Cloud Provider added to your Profile which will look as follows

Once cloud provider or team(given below) is created or shared(given below), you can see the parameters by clicking
on the 'green eye icon'.

II. Configurations

You can create a configuration which takes as input a cloud provider name (I above) and a deployment parameter name (IV below), which can be made use of while deploying applications
To add configuration, click on the 'Configurations' tab To the right is a '+' symbol which will allow you to add a Configuration. You can see that the cloud provider name 'TSISTACK',
was the one created before using I above and the deployment parameter name is the one created using III below

Click on 'SAVE' to save the deployment parameters. Now the deployment parameters are displayed as follows

You can click on the 'red bin icon', to delete the deployment parameters.

III. Deployment Parameters

To add deployment parameters (you can create a group of deployment parameters at one go), click on the 'Deployment Parameters' tab in your Profile Page.
To the right is a '+' symbol which will allow you to add a deployment parameter.

Click on 'SAVE' to save the deployment parameters. Now the deployment parameters are displayed as follows

You can click on the 'red bin icon', to delete the deployment parameters.

IV. Teams

To add teams, click on the 'Teams' tab in your Profile Page.
To the right is a '+' symbol which will allow you to add a team. Enter the team name and click on 'Save'.


You can add team members, by clicking on the team now, which is displayed like

You will find the 'add people icon' to the right,click on it, enter the email of the person, and click on 'ADD' as shown

Click on 'ADD' to keep adding more members. Now the team and its members are displayed as follows

You can click on the 'red cross icon', to remove members from team.

Sharing Cloud providers

You can share your credentials, with another person or a team. To share Cloud Providers, click on the 'Cloud Providers' you want
to share. To the right is a symbol which will allow you to share a Cloud Provider.


You can enter the email of a person you want to share with or the name
of the team you want to share with and click on 'Share'.

Note: The email you want to share with or the team you want to share with should be,
registered cloud portal accounts.

Sharing Applications

You can share your application, with another person or a team. To share Applications, click on the 'Applications' you want to share.
To the right is a symbol which will allow you to share a Cloud Provider.


You can enter the email of a person you want to share with or the name of the team you want to share with and click on 'Share'.

Note: The email you want to share with or the team you want to share with should be,
registered cloud portal accounts.

Deploying Applications

You can deploy your application, to be run on a cloud provider of your choice. Click on 'Repository' and click on the Application, you want to deploy as follows.
This will then take you to the deployment page. You have to choose the cloud provider, you want your application to run on.
For this from the top left corner, click on 'Select CloudProvider' and select a cloud provider, which you have defined.

If you have deployment parameters defined for your application and you have also defined a configuration providing those, you can use it.
Under 'Choose Configurations' a list of Configurations you have defined will appear and you can use one of them.
If the configuration you have chosen, has a deployment parameter matching the deployment parameters for the application,
the value is displayed like

Click on the 'green eye' icon, to view the 'Deployment Parameters' if any. Enter the inputs for the application if any under 'Inputs', and then click on the 'Deploy' button as follows

Once the application is deployed, you can click on 'Deployments' from the side navigation bar, to view the deployments.
