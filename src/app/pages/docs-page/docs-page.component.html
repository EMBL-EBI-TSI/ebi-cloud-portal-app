Introduction

This section is to help you to use the portal demonstrated with help of screenshots.

Welcome Page

This is the usual welcome or start up page. To the top right corner you will find 'Login' link.

Single Sign On

If you don't have an ELIXIR associated account, you need to create one using one of the supported providers (Google, institutional account, etc.).

This involves email verification, and finishes with a "We've got your registration" message from ELIXIR. When you first log in to the EBI Cloud Portal, you will first be asked if you allow your home institution (or Google, etc) to release your data to ELIXIR, where you can choose to allow/disallow the release of your data to the EBI Authentication Gateway.

Let's review the process step by step

Here you make use of the SSO (single sign on) button.

You can sign in using Google, Linkedin, ORCID accounts or your institution login.

Say for example, you have entered your institution name, you will be taken to another page, where you enter your login details you use in you institution to as follows

Enter the login details and click on 'Login'. You will be redirected to the portal page.

Profile Page

Click on , a side navigation bar appears as follows

Click on 'Profile' which will then take you to profile page where you can add, share and remove Cloud Providers, Configurations, Deployment Parameters, and Teams.

I. Cloud providers

To add Cloud Providers, click on the 'Cloud Providers' tab in you Profile Page. To the right is a '+' symbol which will allow you to add a Cloud Provider.

You can add Cloud Provider Parameters , by clicking on the '+' next to the Parameters. To add more parameters just click on '+'. Then hit on 'ADD' button to save the information. You will be able to see the Cloud Provider added to your Profile.

Once cloud provider or team (given below) is created or shared (given below), you can see the parameters by clicking on the 'green eye icon'.

II. Configurations

You can create a Configuration which takes as input a Cloud Provider name and a Deployment Parameter name, which can be made use of while deploying applications. To add a Configuration, click on the 'Configurations' tab To the right is a '+' symbol which will allow you to add a Configuration. The Cloud Provider and other entries allow you to select from your previously added elements.

Click on 'ADD' to save.

You can click on the 'red bin icon', to delete the deployment parameters.

III. Deployment Parameters

Deployment Parameters can be added in a similar way to the previous entities. Click on 'ADD' to save the deployment parameters as usual. You can also click on the 'bin icon', to delete the deployment parameters.

IV. Teams

Teams are an essential part to the EBI Cloud Portal. Through teams we can share items such as applications or Configurations with its members. To add teams, go to the 'Teams' tab in your Profile Page.

Use the '+' button to open the add team dialog. Enter the team name and click on 'ADD'.

You can add team members, by clicking on your new team card. that will take you to the team details page.

There you will find the 'add people icon' to the right, click on it, enter the email of the person, and click on 'ADD'. Click on 'ADD' to keep adding more members.

You can click on the 'red cross icon', to remove members from team.

Sharing items iwthin teams is done through the card of the item we want to share (e.g. Application, Configuration, etc).

Repository Page

Click on , the side navigation bar appears. Click on Repository which will then take you to repository page where you can manage Applications.

To add applications, click on the '+' symbol in Repository page

You can then enter the repository URI. The application should have a file named MANIFEST.json with parameters, like the one given below https://github.com/jadianes/be-application-test. After entering the repository path, click on 'ADD'.

Your application will be added to the list.

Sharing

Sharing Cloud providers

You can share your cloud credentials, with other users within a team. To share Cloud Providers, click on the 'Cloud Provider' you want to share. To the right there is a symbol which will allow you to share a this item.

A dialog will pop up where you can enter the the name of the team you want to share with. Then click on 'Share'.

Sharing Applications

You can share your applications in the same way. Click on the one you want to share. To the right there is the symbol which will allow you to share it.

Cloud deployment

Deploying Applications

You can deploy your application, to be run on a cloud provider of your choice. Click on 'Repository' and click on the Application, you want to deploy as follows.
This will then take you to the deployment page. You have to choose the cloud provider, you want your application to run on.
For this from the top left corner, click on 'Select CloudProvider' and select a cloud provider, which you have defined.

If you have deployment parameters defined for your application and you have also defined a configuration providing those, you can use it.
Under 'Choose Configurations' a list of Configurations you have defined will appear and you can use one of them.
